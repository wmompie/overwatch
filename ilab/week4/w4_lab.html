What has been done to this page.

I have chosen two designs: Change on Hover & Slide in Content. On this page you will see three places where I have chosen to implement these designs.

The first being the Navigation Menu. I changed the look of it to be oval by default and when hovered to become a rectangle and also to change coloring/border. This option also works in the menus for the sub menus. The second area being the aside when hovered over to change the coloring of the background and the words so it may still be legible while having decent contrast to one another.

The final area where I have chosen to use my second design Slide in Content can be seen to the left. I added a minor border radius and also decided to make the background color match the words in the navigation menu to keep the coloring consistent.

**NOTE: The navigation menu will not take you to any other page on purpose. This page is only for an iLab assignment and is strictly for demonstration purposes. These designs have also been implemented in the main website as well.**
